BBEGinator is a tool designed to make better DnD 5th Edition combat encounters (because CR sucks)

Create encounters: create an encounter with as many monsters as you'd like. Select, display, and download your monster stat blocks

Search for monsters: filter for monsters matching your desired criteria

Recommend mosters by party: input some basic information about your DnD party (class and level), as well as your desired combat encounter difficulty, to receive monster encounter suggestions

Want to add homebrew? We support that :). Choose the "download as a monster" option and upload to tetra-cube's DnD 5e Statblock Generator!
